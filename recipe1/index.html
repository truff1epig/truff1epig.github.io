Vegan Adobo

Ingredients

  • 3-5 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 of flour
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce or liquid aminos
  • 1/2 apple cider vinegar
  • 16-20 oz. super firm tofu
  • 2-4 cups of cooked white or brown rice

Directions

  1. Dice the onion and mince the garlic.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan on low heat. Put the onion and garlic in when oil is warm. Stir occasionally until onions have browned.
  3. Press tofu and cut into small cubes. Toss the cubes with flour until each is coated.
  4. Fry the tofu with the onions and garlic until the cedges of each cube are crispy and brown.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and apple cider vinegar. Add the bay leaves, making sure they are submerged in the sauce. Let simmer for 20-45 minutes.
  6. Serve over rice and enjoy!
